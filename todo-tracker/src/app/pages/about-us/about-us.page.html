 Our Story

Founded in 2024, Todo Tracker was born from the need for a simple, no-fuss task management application. We noticed that many productivity apps were becoming overly complex, so we decided to create a solution that focuses on what truly matters: getting things done.

Our team of passionate developers continues to improve the application based on user feedback, ensuring that Todo Tracker remains the go-to choice for simple and effective task management.
